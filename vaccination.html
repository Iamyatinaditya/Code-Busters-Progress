Vaccine Companies

1. Bharat Biotech:

Bharat Biotech International Limited is a global biotechnology firm based in Hyderabad, India, that specialises in drug discovery, development, and manufacturing of vaccines, biotherapeutics, pharmaceuticals, and health-care products.




2. Pfizer:

Pfizer Inc., based on 42nd Street in Manhattan, New York City, is an American multinational pharmaceutical and biotechnology firm. In 1849, two German immigrants, Charles Pfizer and his relative Charles F. Erhart, founded the corporation in New York.




3. AstraZeneca

AstraZeneca plc is a British-Swedish multinational pharmaceutical and biotechnology corporation headquartered in Cambridge, England's Cambridge Biomedical Campus.




Pfizer:

Pfizer has a long history of vaccine development and research. They have played a major role in eliminating or nearly eliminating deadly infectious diseases like smallpox, polio and COVID 19 globally


AstraZeneca:

The Oxford/AstraZeneca COVID-19 vaccine, codenamed AZD1222, is a viral vector vaccine for COVID-19 prevention that is commercialised under the trade names Covishield and Vaxzevria, among others.


COVAXIN:

Immuno-potentiators, also known as vaccine adjuvants, are added to the vaccine to improve and raise its immunogenicity. COVAXIN is one of them. It's a two-dose vaccine that's administered 28 days apart.
